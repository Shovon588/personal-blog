How to change your username?

1. Go to admin.

2. Under AUTHENTICATION AND AUTHORIZATION tab click on Users.

3. There shold be one your username, click on that.

4. In this page you can see Username under Change user tab.

5. Change your username and click on save.

6. Your username will be updated.

How to change your password?

1. Go to admin.

2. Under AUTHENTICATION AND AUTHORIZATION tab click on Users.

3. There shold be one your username, click on that.

4. In this page you can see Password under Change user tab.

5. There you can see something like this "Raw passwords are not stored, so there is no way to see this userâ€™s password, but you can change the password using this form."

6. Click on this form, change password and save.

How to add new user?

If you want to add multiple user in your blog you can do that too. Suppose you want to allow another user to post on your blog then you can add an user and give the credentials to them.

1. Go to admin.

2. Under AUTHENTICATION AND AUTHORIZATION tab click on Users.

3. Click on ADD USER.

4. Setup the username and password and click save.

5. This will add a new user.

How to change content in about page?

1. Go to admin.

2. Under BLOG tab click on Abouts.

3. There should be only one item which is you current about. Click on it.

4. Change or modify this and click save.

5. Your about will be updated.
